Travel Tips

Packing Light

Take versatile clothing layers, pack by outfits, and use packing cubes. Leave heavy items at home — you can often buy small things locally.

Safety & Documents

Always keep a scanned copy of your passport and tickets in cloud storage or email. Carry a small first-aid kit and know emergency numbers of the region.

Local Transport

Check local transport options before the trip (bus schedules, shared taxis). For rural places, download offline maps and save key coordinates.

Respect Nature

Follow marked trails, do not leave trash, and avoid disturbing wildlife. In fragile areas (mountain lakes, dunes), use local guides when recommended.
